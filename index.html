Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about DevConf 2026.

What is DevConf 2026?

DevConf 2026 is a three-day developer conference featuring expert speakers, workshops, networking sessions, and hands-on learning experiences.

Who can attend this conference?

Anyone interested in technology, including students, software developers, engineers, founders, and IT professionals.

How can I purchase a ticket?

You can choose your preferred ticket from the pricing section and complete the registration process online.

Will workshops be included?

Yes. Workshop access depends on your selected ticket type. Pro and Team tickets include premium workshops.

Will I receive a participation certificate?

Yes. Every registered attendee will receive a digital participation certificate after the event.

Can I get updates before the conference?

Yes. Register your interest and we'll send updates about speakers, schedules, tickets, and conference announcements.
